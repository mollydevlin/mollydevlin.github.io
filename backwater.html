Backwater is a final year production, produced by the graduate students of the National Film School in IADT 2018. The film is set in rural Ireland following the life of a young boy who has put his life on pause to care for his father who suffers with alzheimer’s.

The meaning of “Backwater” is a situation or a place where no progress or development has been made. In these film titles, I did not want to give too much away, but moreso set a mood and a lead up to the film. I wanted to communicate the feeling of being stuck, unkept, rural and isolation while also referring to fragmented memories of alzheimer’s.
