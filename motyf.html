Motyf is an exciting vertual typography conference held in Dublin in October 2018. The breif was to create a modern, vertual and striking visual identity for the annual festival to visually communicate what the conference is all about.

For this project I wanted to express the modern side of the conference landing into dublin 2018, I wanted the visual language to look like it's moving at all times to compliment the "Vertual Typography" aspect.
